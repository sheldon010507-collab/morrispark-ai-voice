# Morris Park FAQ

## What is Morris Park?

Morris Park is a Glasgow commercial workspace location operated by H. Morris
Group. It is suitable for businesses looking for practical workspace with useful
road access and on-site facilities.

## Where is it?

Morris Park is at 37 Rosyth Road, Glasgow G5 0YE. It is located next to the M74.

## Do you have parking?

Yes. Morris Park lists on-site parking as one of its facilities.

## What facilities are available?

Facilities include high-speed Wi-Fi, on-site parking, CCTV security, shower
facilities, and an on-site cafe. 24-hour access and overnight security are also
listed where applicable.

## What spaces are available?

The public availability page lists a 610 sq ft work zone, a 473 sq ft two-room
space with reception area, yard space, and MOT vehicle parking from £450/month.
Real-time availability should be confirmed with the Morris Park team.

## Can I book a viewing?

Yes. The AI guide should collect the visitor's name, best email or phone, the
kind of space they want, and a preferred viewing time, then save the lead for the
Morris Park team.
